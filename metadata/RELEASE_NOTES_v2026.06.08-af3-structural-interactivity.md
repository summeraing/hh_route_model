## AF3 archive contents

The AF3 archive contains 19 representative model CIF files, 19 AF3 confidence JSON outputs, 19 ranking-score CSV files, integrated interface-metric tables and interpretation-boundary metadata.

## Boundary

The AF3 screen is provided as a gate-calibrated structural-feasibility support layer. It is not interpreted as direct reconstruction of ancestral binding interfaces and does not include proprietary AF3 model parameters.

## Excluded

Manuscript files, cover letters, journal upload files, final figures, figure-design working files and editorial planning materials remain excluded from this public repository.
